REDUCE, REUSE & RECYCLE

Saving our plannet

Recycling is an important way to save our planet and preserve its resources. By reducing waste, conserving natural resources, and reducing energy consumption, recycling helps to minimize our impact on the environment. By sorting and cleaning recyclables, we can ensure that materials are properly processed and reused. Additionally, by buying products made from recycled materials, we can support the recycling industry and reduce the demand for virgin materials. These small actions can make a big difference in creating a more sustainable future for ourselves and future generations.

Clean environment

Recycling can play a crucial role in maintaining a clean environment. By reducing waste in landfills, recycling can help prevent soil and water pollution caused by waste disposal. Recycling also reduces the need for raw materials, which helps preserve natural habitats and ecosystems. By reducing energy consumption, recycling can help reduce greenhouse gas emissions, which can contribute to air pollution and climate change. Furthermore, by properly sorting and cleaning recyclables, we can prevent contamination and improve the quality of recycled materials. These benefits show how recycling can contribute to a cleaner, healthier environment for all.

Helping the community

Giving away extra food can help others in several ways. First and foremost, it can help alleviate hunger and food insecurity in your community, especially among those who may not have the means to purchase or access enough food for themselves or their families. It can also reduce food waste and promote sustainability by diverting food from landfills. Additionally, donating food can foster a sense of community and generosity, both for the giver and the recipient. Overall, giving away extra food can have a positive impact on both individuals and the wider society, helping to address pressing social and environmental issues.

What we do and how we do it.

Welcome to TRASH2 Treasure, where we are dedicated to promoting sustainability and reducing waste through recycling plastic, old fur niture, and electronic items, as well as donating extra food instead of throwing it away.

What is our goal?

Our mission is to promote sustainability and protect the environment by reducing waste. Our team provide's customized solutions for clients to meet their sustainability goals, including collecting, transporting, and processing plastic waste, old furniture, and electronic items. The company uses advanced recycling technologies to ensure that materials are recycled efficiently and effectively.

Increasing morals in a community

We connect individuals with local organizations to donate excess food and make a positive impact on the community. We wish to reduce food waste while ensuring that those in need have access to nutritious meals. We believe that by promoting a culture of generosity and compassion, we can help to increase morals within the community. Through our platform, users can easily donate extra food to us which we later donate to local food banks and charities. We work closely with our partner organizations to ensure that donations are distributed efficiently and effectively. Join us in our efforts to create a more sustainable and compassionate community.

What do we do with the waste?

After the pickup, we transport the waste to our factories, where we sort the waste into different categories; recyclable to non -recyclable. We use advanced recycling technologies to ensure materials are recycled efficiently and effectively. From plastic to metallic to old furniture to paper to electronic waste, we try our best to recycle as much as possible so that fewer items end up in landfills.

What actions we take to store and deliver the food to dontation centers?

We follow and take every precaution under the guidelines of the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India. During the transport, food gets stored in sterile and hygienic containers. Later the food gets sent to our various facilities where it is stored in a cool and clean environment. After this, we contact our partnered organizations and food banks and deliver the items to them. We oversee the entire procedure and make sure every action is taken adequately to deliver the nutritious meals.
